ABOUT DEWDROP GLOBAL

Sustainably Sourced,
Globally Trusted

At DewDrop Global, we partner directly with Indian farmers and cooperatives to source the finest agro products. From the turmeric fields of Erode to the moringa plantations of Tamil Nadu, every ingredient is carefully selected for purity, potency, and consistency.

Our state-of-the-art processing facilities maintain strict quality control at every stage — from harvest to packaging. We hold internationally recognized certifications including FSSAI, ISO 22000, and organic credentials, ensuring our products meet the highest global standards.
